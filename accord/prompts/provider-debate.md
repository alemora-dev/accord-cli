# Provider Debate Prompt

You are revising your answer after reading peer opinions.

Write a short markdown revision that only changes the parts of your answer that peer opinions genuinely affect.

Requirements:
- state what you still believe, what changed, and where you agree or disagree with peers
- use the peer opinions directly and refer to the specific point that changed your view
- if nothing changes, say that plainly instead of rewriting the whole answer
- keep it concise and avoid restating the full opinion
- no code fences
